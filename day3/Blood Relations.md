# Question 1

Aman said,

> "The man in the photograph is the son of my grandfather's only son."

Who is the man in the photograph to Aman?

### Options

A. Brother  
B. Father  
C. Uncle  
D. Cousin

---

## Solution

### Step 1

Grandfather's only son = Aman's father

### Step 2

Son of Aman's father = Aman himself

Family Tree:

```text
Grandfather
     |
   Father
     |
   Aman
```

### Answer

**A. Brother (or Aman himself depending on interpretation)**

> [!TIP]
> Whenever you see "grandfather's only son", immediately replace it with "father".

# Question 2

P is the brother of Q.

R is the sister of Q.

T is the father of P.

How is T related to R?

### Options

A. Uncle  
B. Father  
C. Grandfather  
D. Brother

---

## Solution

Given:

```text
      T
      |
   --------
   |      |
   P      Q
          |
          R
```

P and Q are siblings.

R is sister of Q.

Therefore all three are children of T.

### Answer

**B. Father**

# Blood Relation Solving Strategy

1. Identify gender first.
2. Underline keywords:
   - Father
   - Mother
   - Son
   - Daughter
   - Brother
   - Sister
3. Draw a tree.
4. Move generation by generation.
5. Find the final relationship.

> [!TIP]
> 90% of Blood Relation questions become easy once converted into a family tree.

### Options

> [!QUESTION]- Show Answer
> 
> **C. Uncle**

> [!NOTE]- Show Explanation
> 
> Step 1:
>

> 
> Step 2:
> 
> Father of my daughter's father = Arun's father

>

# Question 6 (Symbol Based)

If:

```text
A + B → A is the father of B
A - B → A is the mother of B
A × B → A is the sister of B

```

What is D's relation to A?

```text
A + B - C × D
```

### Options

A. Aunt  
B. Daughter  
C. Niece  
D. Sister

---

> [!QUESTION]- Show Answer
> 
> **B. Daughter**

> [!NOTE]- Show Explanation
> 
> ```text
> A + B
> A is father of B
> 
> B - C
> B is mother of C
> 
> C × D
> C is sister of D
> ```
> 
> Family Tree:
> 
> ```text
>       A
>       |
>       B
>       |
>    -------
>    |     |
>    C     D
> ```
> 
> D is also a child of B.

# Pattern 1: Only Daughter / Only Son

## Question

```text

How is the man related to the woman?
```

### Options

A. Brother  
B. Son

<details>
<summary>Answer & Explanation</summary>

```text
Only daughter of my father = Me
```

**Answer: B. Son**
