# Pattern 1: Final Facing Direction

## What They Ask

```text
Which direction is the person facing now?
```

### Common Clues

```text
Left Turn
Right Turn
Left Turn
Right Turn
```

Only track orientation.

---

## Representative Question

```text
A person started walking towards South.

After walking some distance,
he turned left.

Then turned right.

Then turned left.

Which direction is he facing now?
```

### Solution

```text
Start → South

Left
↓

East

Right
↓

South

Left
↓

East
```

<details>
<summary>Answer</summary>

```text
East
```

</details>

# Pattern 2: Final Position from Starting Point

## What They Ask

```text
In which direction is he from the starting point?
```

### Shortcut

```text
North = +Y
South = -Y

East = +X
West = -X
```

Cancel opposites.

---

## Representative Question

```text
Walk 7 km East

Turn Left
Walk 4 km

Turn Right
Walk 5 km

Turn Right
Walk 4 km

Where is he from the starting point?
```

### Solution

```text
East = 7 + 5 = 12

North = 4
South = 4

Cancel
```

Remaining:

```text
12 km East
```

<details>
<summary>Answer</summary>

```text
East
```

</details>

# Pattern 3: Shortest Distance

## What They Ask

```text
What is the shortest distance from the starting point?
```

---

## Representative Question

```text
A person walks:

9 m East

12 m South

Find shortest distance.
```

### Diagram

```text
      |
      |\
   12 | \ 15
      |  \
      |___\
        9
```

### Formula

```text
Distance²
=
Base² + Height²
```

### Calculation

```text
9² + 12²

81 + 144

225

√225 = 15
```

<details>
<summary>Answer</summary>

```text
15 m
```

</details>

# Pattern 4: Sunrise, Sunset & Shadow

## Golden Rules

```text
Sunrise → East

Shadow → West
```

```text
Sunset → West

Shadow → East
```

```text
12 Noon

No Shadow
```

---

## Representative Question

```text
Morning.

A person's shadow is exactly to his right.

Which direction is he facing?
```

### Solution

```text
Morning

Shadow = West
```

If West is on his right:

```text
He must be facing South
```

<details>
<summary>Answer</summary>

```text
South
```

</details>

# Pattern 5: Relative Position

## What They Ask

```text
A is North-West of B

C is South-West of B

Find C with respect to A
```

Always draw.

---

## Representative Question

```text
Mall is North-West of Market.

School is South-West of Market.

Find School from Mall.
```

### Diagram

```text
Mall

   *
    \
     \
Market

     /
    /
   *

School
```

### Observation

```text
Both are equally West.

School is below Mall.
```

<details>
<summary>Answer</summary>

```text
South
```

</details>

# Pattern 6: Diagonal Directions

## Direction Coordinates

| Direction | Coordinate |
|------------|------------|
| NE | (+1,+1) |
| NW | (-1,+1) |
| SE | (+1,-1) |
| SW | (-1,-1) |

---

## Representative Question

```text
P is North-West of K

R is South-West of K

Find R with respect to P
```

### Coordinates

```text
P = (-1,+1)

R = (-1,-1)
```

Difference:

```text
Same X

R is lower
```

<details>
<summary>Answer</summary>

```text
South
```

</details>
