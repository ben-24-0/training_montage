# Coding-Decoding Patterns with Real Examples

> Examples below are taken from the questions and notes you provided.

---

# 1. Direct Letter Shift (-1)

## Example

```text
COULD → BNTKC
```

### Observation

| C | O | U | L | D |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| B | N | T | K | C |

Rule:

```text
Each letter = Previous alphabet
```

```text
C → B
O → N
U → T
L → K
D → C
```

### Similar Question

```text
MOULDING → ?
```

> [!QUESTION]- Show Answer
>
> ```text
> M → L
> O → N
> U → T
> L → K
> D → C
> I → H
> N → M
> G → F
> ```
>
> ```text
> LNTKCHMF
> ```

---

# 2. Reverse + Middle Shift

## Example

```text
COMPUTER → RFUVQNPC
```

### Step 1

Reverse

```text
COMPUTER
↓
RETUPMOC
```

### Step 2

Keep first and last letters

```text
R ETUPMO C
```

### Step 3

Shift middle letters by +1

```text
ETUPMO
↓
FUVQNP
```

### Final

```text
RFUVQNPC
```

### Similar Question

```text
MEDICINE → ?
```

> [!QUESTION]- Show Answer
>
> Reverse:
>
> ```text
> ENICIDEM
> ```
>
> Keep:
>
> ```text
> E NICIDE M
> ```
>
> Shift +1:
>
> ```text
> OJDJEF
> ```
>
> Final:
>
> ```text
> EOJDJEFM
> ```

# 3. Opposite Alphabet Coding

## Example

```text
YEARLY → BVZIOB
```

### Rule

```text
A ↔ Z
B ↔ Y
C ↔ X
...
```

### Mapping

| Y | E | A | R | L | Y |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| B | V | Z | I | O | B |

### Similar Question

```text
ANNUAL → ?
```

> [!QUESTION]- Show Answer
>
> ```text
> A → Z
> N → M
> N → M
> U → F
> A → Z
> L → O
> ```
>
> ```text
> ZMMFZO
> ```

| Letter | Position | Code |
|----------|----------|----------|
| E | 5 | 5 |
| A | 1 | 1 |
| S | 19 | 1+9 = 1 |
| Y | 25 | 2+5 = 7 |

### Answer

```text
5117
```

# 5. Sum of Letter Positions

## Example

```text
SPECIAL → 65
```

### Calculation

```text
S = 19
P = 16
E = 5
C = 3
I = 9
A = 1
L = 12
```

```text
19+16+5+3+9+1+12
= 65
```

### Similar Question

```text
CONNECT → ?
```

> [!QUESTION]- Show Answer
>
> ```text
> C = 3
> O = 15
> N = 14
> N = 14
> E = 5
> C = 3
> T = 20
> ```
>
> ```text
> 3+15+14+14+5+3+20
> = 74
> ```

# 6. Product of Positions

## Example

```text
ISSUE → 341145
```

### Calculation

```text
I = 9
S = 19
S = 19
U = 21
E = 5
```

```text
9 × 19 × 19 × 21 × 5
= 341145
```

### Similar Question

```text
DATES → ?
```

> [!QUESTION]- Show Answer
>
> ```text
> D = 4
> A = 1
> T = 20
> E = 5
> S = 19
> ```
>
> ```text
> 4 × 1 × 20 × 5 × 19
> = 7600
> ```
