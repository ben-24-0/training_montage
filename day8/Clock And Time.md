## Question 1

```text
Find the mirror image of the clock when the time is 01:40.
```

A. 11:20  
B. 10:22  
C. 10:20  
D. 11:22

<details>
<summary>Answer</summary>

```text
Mirror Time

= 11:60 - 01:40

= 10:20
```

**Answer: C**

</details>

---

## Question 2

```text
A clock shows 7:00 AM.

By how much angle will the hour hand rotate
when the clock shows 9:00 AM?
```

A. 40°  
B. 60°  
C. 45°  
D. 90°

<details>
<summary>Answer</summary>

```text
2 hours

1 hour = 30°

2 × 30 = 60°
```

**Answer: B**

</details>

---

</details>

## Question 4

```text
Today is Saturday.

After 43 days it will be?
```

A. Wednesday  
B. Friday  
C. Sunday  
D. Tuesday

<details>
<summary>Answer</summary>

```text
43 = 42 + 1

42 days = 6 weeks

Same day = Saturday

+1 = Sunday
```

**Answer: C**

</details>

## Question 7

```text
What is the angle between the hands
of a clock at 7:30?
```

A. 32°  
B. 43°  
C. 54°  
D. 45°

<details>
<summary>Answer</summary>

```text
Hour hand

= 7 × 30 + 15

= 225°

Minute hand

= 30 × 6

= 180°
```

```text
225 - 180

= 45°
```

**Answer: D**

</details>

## Question 13

```text
A watch gains 5 seconds
in 3 minutes.

It is set correctly at 8 AM.

What will it show at 10 PM
the same day?
```

<details>
<summary>Answer</summary>

```text
8 AM → 10 PM

= 14 hours
```

Gain per hour:

```text
100 sec
```

Gain in 14 hours:

```text
1400 sec

= 23 min 20 sec
```

```text
10:23:20 PM
```

</details>

---

## Question 14

```text
A clock ticks 5 times
in 40 seconds.

How long will it tick
12 times?
```

<details>
<summary>Answer</summary>

```text
5 ticks

= 4 intervals
```

```text
40/4

= 10 sec per interval
```

```text
12 ticks

= 11 intervals
```

```text
11 × 10

= 110 sec
```

**Answer: 110 sec**

</details>

---

## Question 15

```text
A clock loses 15 minutes
every 24 hours.

It is set correctly at
10 AM Monday.

When it shows 4 AM Sunday,
what is the actual time?
```

<details>
<summary>Answer</summary>

```text
Incorrect elapsed time

= 114 hours
```

Clock runs:

```text
95/96 speed
```

Actual elapsed time:

```text
114 × 96/95

= 115.2 hours
```

```text
115 hours 12 min
```

Actual time:

```text
5:12 AM Sunday
```

</details>
